About Me

Brief: Hi! My name is Will. I'm a proud computer nerd with a love for coding, computer hardware, and video games. My life revolves around the computer, in some way or another. I have a lot of interests, so finding the time to sit down and grind out code can be difficult. It usually comes to me in waves, and I'll get an urge to work on a project, either because I feel like it or because of guilt for not coding.

What I Know: There's a lot of stuff that I've dabbled in at some point. I've tried a lot of OO languages as well as a little bit of markup and databases. The languages I know best are Java and C#. Languages I've dabbled with a little bit of Python, JS, BASIC, and C. I've got a fairly good grasp of HTML and CSS. Most of my work is done in Unity, which is a free game engine. No, I don't release half-baked crap that has a Unity watermark on it! This explains why I spend most of my time in C#.

Education: I've been in Computer Science classes since freshman year, and have taken courses through Boise State University. I will be a freshman at BSU next year, but I will be in my 200 classes for Computer Science.
